Custom Integrations

You can integrate your Zoho Services with various cloud services such as Twitter, Google, Salesforce etc., to perform various actions in them directly from your Zoho Service. The integration between Zoho Services and external services takes place by adding a connection for each service. The connections can be utilized using the invoke URL task, which leverages the Connections to perform the integration. To sum it up, the following two things are involved in performing a successful integration:

  • Connections - Connections are used to specify the third-party service with which your Zoho Service will be integrated, along with other details like the Connection Name, Authentication Type, etc. 
  • Invoke Url - The Invoke Url task is used to integrate your Zoho service with third-party apps and perform required actions, through Connections.
