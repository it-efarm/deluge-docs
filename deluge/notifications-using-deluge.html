Notifications using Deluge

Notifications are messages such as alerts or warnings, usually used to notify a user whenever an event occurs. When notification scripts are executed, custom notifications are sent to an email address/phone number, or to a particular device in case of push notifications. For example, you can send notifications to inform users that their submission or update was successfully received. You can keep everyone updated with notifications about the latest data coming into or going out of your Zoho Service using the following options:

  • Push Notification
  • Email
  • Send SMS task
  • SMS Integrations
